Build Graph for Xcode

The essential tool to analyze compilation time of multi-modules app

Features

Timings

Find out long tasks and strange places: thin waterfall of tasks, blocking tasks, long assets compilation and other types of problems

Dependencies

When you have detected strange modules check links to another module to understand the behaviour of compilator

Filters

Choose necessary type of tasks to analyze specified behaviour

Details

Check details of every module: what kind of task is performed, how long does it take

Easy setup

Download, launch, give access to Derived Data. Vualya!

Dark mode

Even on this site: switch the setting and enjoy
